We provide a variety of tours

We are a licensed tour guide service dedicated to providing unforgettable experiences on the beautiful island of Mahé. With its breathtaking beaches, from the tranquil shores of Beau Vallon to the hidden gems along the coastline, Mahé offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and serenity. Our tours are designed to be fun, informative, and stress-free, ensuring you can fully immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes and vibrant culture of Seychelles. Let us guide you through this tropical paradise and create memories that will last a lifetime! Arrive stress-free with our prompt and professional airport pickup service, ensuring you're greeted and transported comfortably to your destination.

  • Full Day Tours - Discover Mahé's best with our Full Day Tours—stunning beaches, cultural highlights, and unforgettable experiences in one seamless journey.
  • Half-Day Tours - Experience Mahé's highlights with our Half Day Tours—perfect for a quick yet memorable adventure.
  • Sunset Tours (Standard) - Unwind with our Sunset Tours, offering a breathtaking view of Mahé's coastline as the sun sets, creating a truly unforgettable experience.
  • Sunset Tours (Premium) - Enjoy a perfect evening with our Sunset Tours, featuring breathtaking views of Mahé's coastline and a glass of champagne to toast the stunning sunset—an unforgettable and luxurious experience.
  • 2-Hour Tours - Make the most of your time with our 2-Hour Tours, offering a quick yet unforgettable glimpse of Mahé's beauty and highlights.
  • Tailored Tours (Full Day or Half Day) - Discover Mahé at your own pace with our Tailored Tours, customized to your interests and preferences. Whether you want to explore beaches, culture, or nature, we craft the perfect itinerary just for you.
